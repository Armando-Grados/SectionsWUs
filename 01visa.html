When seeking entry into the United States, it is highly advised to consult with an immigration law attorney to determine the right visa for your needs. It is important to note that immigration visas vary, and even though you might qualify under several visas, a knowledgeable immigration law attorney can guide you toward the visa that would best suit your circumstance. While some are able to obtain entry through their relation to family members, others are fortunate to work for an employer that facilitates entry. O-1A visa Subclassification

The O-1 visa is intended for individuals with extraordinary abilities or achievements within their field.

Field of Science

The O-1A visa is for individuals with extraordinary abilities in science, education, business, or athletics. It's prestigious and reserved for those at the top of their fields, showcasing exceptional expertise or achievements.

Applicants for the O-1A visa must meet at least three of these conditions

  • Scholarly articles in professional journals
  • Work featured in mainstream media, professional publications, or trade publications
  • Original contributions of major significance in their professional field
  • Scholarly articles in professional journals
  • Participation as a judge (either individually or on a panel) of others’ work in the same field
  • Role in an essential capacity for an organization with a distinguished reputation
  • A high salary or other indication of an individual’s value to an organization, proven by contracts or other documentation
  • Membership in professional associations that require outstanding achievements as recognized by experts in the field

Applicants for the O-1B visa must meet at least three of these conditions

  • National or international recognition for professional achievements
  • Record of commercial or critically acclaimed successes
  • Recognition from organizations, government agencies, critics, or recognized experts
  • Record of commercial or critically acclaimed successes
  • Scheduled to perform in a lead, critical, or starring role for an organization or establishment with a distinguished reputation
  • Have performed or be scheduled to perform services as a starring participant in productions that have a distinguished reputation
